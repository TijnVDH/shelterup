Shelter Experience

The ADE program starts at Shelter on Wednesday with the return of Amsterdam based music label PIV Records. The pioneering sound of the Dutch/Indonesian imprint has become a global reference over the past years with top chart releases and many artists travelling the globe, pioneering the label's identity.

Known for their unique atmosphere with warm, energizing grooves it's the perfect start of your ADE journey with appearances of UK's finest Archie Hamilton and East End Dubs, joined by PIV founder Prunk and residents DJOKO and Iris Menza.
